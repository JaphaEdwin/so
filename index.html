FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes.

How long does the placement process take?

The typical placement process takes 2-4 months from application to departure, depending on visa processing times, documentation requirements, and job matching. We work efficiently to minimize waiting time while ensuring all procedures are properly completed.

What are your service fees?

We operate on a transparent pay-as-you-go model with fees split across different stages. Exact costs vary based on destination country, job type, and specific requirements. Contact us for a detailed, personalized quote with no hidden charges.

Do you provide visa assistance?

Yes, comprehensive visa assistance is included in our services. We handle documentation preparation, application submission, follow-ups, and provide guidance throughout the visa approval process for all destination countries.

What happens if I don't get placed?

We offer a money-back guarantee. If we're unable to secure a placement as per our agreement, you'll receive a full refund of service fees. We're committed to your success and stand behind our services 100%.

Which countries do you place candidates in?

We have active partnerships in over 25 countries, with strong presence in UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, Canada, UK, and several European nations. Contact us to discuss specific country preferences and opportunities.
